Tracking Methods

Body Overview

The Body Overview section provides a comprehensive summary of your current physical state. It includes key metrics such as weight, BMI, and overall calorie consumption, offering a clear snapshot of your health progress and areas needing attention.

Daily Target

The Daily Target section sets personalized goals for water intake, calorie consumption, and physical activity. These targets are tailored to help you achieve optimal health and maintain a balanced lifestyle.

Meal Plan

The Meal Plan section provides a detailed breakdown of your daily meals, including calorie counts and nutritional information. It helps you plan your breakfast, lunch, and dinner to ensure a balanced diet.

Drinking Tracker

The Drinking Tracker section monitors your daily water intake, ensuring you stay hydrated throughout the day. Adequate hydration is essential for overall health, affecting everything from energy levels to skin health

Daily Exercise

The Daily Exercise section outlines your workout routines and tracks your physical activity. It includes recommendations for exercises tailored to your fitness level and goals, ensuring you stay active and healthy.

Sleep Tracker

The Sleep Tracker section monitors your sleep patterns and quality. It provides insights into your sleeping habits, helping you understand how well you rest and how it affects your overall health.
